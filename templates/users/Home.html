About Us

The Farmers Registry System (FRS) is an agriculture database of farmers created by the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock. Farmers Registration System is a mandatory registration system that records farmer information at a central database that supports inquiries to ensure that agriculture support is traceable, auditable and reportable.
