The Farmers Registry System (FRS) is an agriculture database of farmers created by the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock. Farmers Registration System is a mandatory registration system that records farmer information at a central database that supports inquiries to ensure that agriculture support is traceable, auditable and reportable.

Farmers Registration System is a holistic collection of system that keeps records of personal information and assets for example land, livestock and inputs of farmers actively engaged in agricultural activities as well as cropping patterns and average yields. Agricultural support is implemented, monitored and audited through Farmers Registration System which also informs the creation of agricultural policies
